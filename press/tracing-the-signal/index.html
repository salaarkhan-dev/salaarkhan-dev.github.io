Tracing the Signal

An after-hours investigation into a flickering bug that only confessed under pressure.

The Lead. A sporadic failure whispered across logs — too quick to hold, too costly to ignore. Our goal: catch it in the act without scaring it off.

“Bugs hide in timing. Make time visible.”

Method

Instrumentation

We added trace spans around suspected critical sections and widened sampling at the edges.

  • Isolate concurrency hotspots
  • Reproduce under load
  • Validate with trace correlation

Visual Evidence

Findings

  1. Contended shared state — a mutex around a hot path serialized requests.
  2. Non-deterministic scheduling — an async retry raced with cache refresh.
  3. Hidden retry loops — exponential backoff amplified pressure at peak.
